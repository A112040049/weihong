Intro

I was born on April 23, 2005, in New Taipei City, Taiwan. Currently, I am 19 years old and studying in the Department of Communication at Shih Hsin University. I have a passion for music and have created some musical works, with a style leaning towards Disco Funk. If you're interested, you can check them out in my MUSIC WORK section.

I also enjoy drawing. I've created a virtual character: The Cat in the Hole. It only has one expression, yet it represents my personality and inner world. If you're fond of this cute and mysterious cat, you can check it out in my ARTWORK section.

Artwork

"The Cat in the Hole" is a cat with only one expression, and no one has ever seen its lower body. It has a history of 7 years, and I started creating "The Cat in the Hole" since the sixth grade of elementary school. I often use this idea to draw birthday cards for my friends. I also create "The Cat in the Hole" for my classmates, incorporating their features such as eyes, hair, nose, and so on.

The most classic piece of "The Cat in the Hole" is "Cat Market". This idea is actually a recreation of a concept from one of my elementary school classmates. In this artwork, numerous cats are crowded together shopping, with convenience stores, clothing stores, fish markets, and more nearby, just like humans bustling around a marketplace for groceries.

Music Work

Actually, I have only been composing music for three years. My first attempt at music composition was in a high school elective course, where I created my first pop song. I incorporated violin and piano with Disco melodies, emphasizing the violin in the transitions to evoke a sense of sadness, while the piano emphasized hopefulness. This song received high praise from professional teachers, which greatly boosted my motivation for music composition. Two years later, I composed two more pieces. The first one utilized small marimba and xylophone, combined with Disco drum rhythms, creating an underground world atmosphere. The second one experimented with Trap drum beats paired with glass synthesizers, producing a dreamy vibe. If you're interested in listening to my music, feel free to click the link below. 1.
